Testing Guide

Follow these steps to explore the full power of Shopbox within your Shopify store.

1

Installation

Install the app from the Shopify App Store. Once installed, it will appear in your "Apps" list in the Shopify Admin.

2

Native Integration

Open the app. It loads directly inside your Shopify admin via App Bridge, so no separate login or dashboard URL is required.

3

Channel Connection

On the main dashboard, click "Connect Channel" to see options for connecting your Instagram Business, Facebook Messenger, and WhatsApp accounts.

4

Automations & Bot Setup

Navigate to "Automations" to view the comment trigger setup and configure your AI DM bot (e.g., auto-replying to "Price?" on Instagram).

5

Post-to-Product Mapping

Navigate to "Post Automation" to test the mapping feature that allows your bot to send direct product checkout links based on specific social posts.

6

Order Management

Navigate to "Orders" to view the dedicated management dashboard for sales converted through social channels.

7

Billing & Pro Features

Select a plan (Starter or Pro) to test the billing flow. Use **Shopify's Test Mode** during the selection process to avoid any real-world charges.

Testing Requirement: While core UI and navigation can be tested without connecting external accounts, full functionality requires a valid Meta (Facebook/Instagram) or WhatsApp Business account connection.
