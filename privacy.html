Privacy Policy Shopbox by Adberry

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

ShopBox ("we", "our", or "us") is a multi-channel commerce automation platform that integrates with Shopify to help merchants manage customer engagement across Instagram and Facebook Messenger. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you install and use the ShopBox Shopify app.

By installing ShopBox, you ("the Merchant") agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Merchant Information

When you install ShopBox, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain and access token
  • Store name and configuration
  • Connected channel credentials (Instagram, Facebook Messenger tokens and account IDs)
  • Billing and subscription status

2.2 Customer Data

Through your store's interactions, we may process:

  • Customer name, phone number, and email address (from orders)
  • Delivery address
  • Order history and status
  • Instagram commenter ID and username
  • Facebook Messenger conversation content
  • Bot conversation state and context

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • IP addresses (stored in audit logs)
  • Webhook event data from Shopify and Meta
  • Timestamps of all interactions

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to operate the ShopBox platform and deliver its features, including automating responses to Instagram comments and sending messages via Facebook Messenger. We do not sell your data or your customers' data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Service Purpose Data Shared
Shopify E-commerce platform integration Store tokens, product and order data
Meta Instagram and Messenger automation Page tokens, account IDs, message content
AWS / RDS Database hosting All stored data
Railway Application hosting Data in transit

5. Data Retention

  • Merchant data is retained for the duration of your subscription and deleted upon a valid shop redact request.
  • Customer data is retained to support your business operations. Personal identifiers are anonymized upon a valid customer redact request.
  • Audit logs are retained for up to 12 months.

6. GDPR Compliance

ShopBox complies with Shopify's GDPR requirements. We support mandatory webhooks for Customer Data Request, Customer Data Erasure (Redact), and Shop Data Erasure (Redact).
